One of the most beautiful countries on earth, Sri Lanka lies like a teardrop-shaped gem off the southern tip of India, so Sri Lanka is often referred to as
"the pearl of the Indian Ocean."

Defined by many as one of the best islands in the world, Sri Lanka is home to many tourists. It has so much variety to offer and is definitely a place you should visit at least once in a lifetime. In this fascinating island, you will have the opportunity to experience and see different things. From beautiful beaches to ancient ruins, from beautiful cities to local temples, there is an amazing variety of wildlife. The good news is that Sri Lanka is on an island that is on the tourist radar.
